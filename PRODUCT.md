# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Poll creators, team leads, and community managers who need to launch and monitor polls quickly. Most sessions are short and action-driven: users want to create a poll, publish it, and then track responses without navigating complex settings first.

## Product Purpose

Open Poll helps creators build, publish, and analyze polls with minimal friction. Success means users can move from idea to live poll in a fast, guided flow, while still having control over key publishing settings.

## Brand Personality

Focused, confident, and efficient. The interface should feel modern and premium, but never flashy at the cost of clarity. The tone should support momentum and reduce hesitation during creation flows.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy admin panels, cluttered card-heavy dashboards, and overly playful visual treatments that distract from task completion. Avoid burying critical publishing choices in dense forms.

## Design Principles

1. Progress first: guide users through clear next steps instead of presenting all controls at once.
2. Creator momentum: keep high-intent actions prominent, with low-friction transitions between stages.
3. Settings on demand: defer advanced configuration to contextual moments such as publish.
4. One surface, one rhythm: align dashboard and create experiences into a coherent creator studio language.
5. Clarity over novelty: visual polish should reinforce comprehension, not compete with it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and focus visibility. Maintain keyboard-reachable action paths for creation and publishing flows. Use concise labels and state text that remain understandable under reduced motion preferences and varied screen sizes.
